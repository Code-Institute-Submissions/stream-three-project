Mother's Nature

There are two things that I hold very dear: the elegance of my country’s nature and my family. It was these two factors that led me to paint Mother’s Nature, which depicts both the physical and the emotional beauty of Africa’s wildlife. This painting stays true to one of my central themes through celebrating the exquisiteness of the giraffe’s form. But, in protecting and grooming its young, the mother portrays an important part of my cherished familial connections.
-Keryn Mason
