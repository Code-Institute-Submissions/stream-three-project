About Artist

Keryn Mason

Hi! I’m Keryn, a South African artist who is completely inspired by my surroundings and the history of my beautiful country. You’ll find a lot of African influence in my work, but I don’t limit myself to African subject matter. In fact, I’ll paint anything that catches my fancy.

I don’t like to inhibit my creativity in any way, so I don’t tend to label my style. But, I will say that my work reflects my adoration of Africa’s wilderness and my close bond with my family.

I live in Port Elizabeth, which is a beautiful city and the perfect place to call home. The slower-paced lifestyle helps me to connect with the peaceful pulse of Africa, and it fuels my inspiration. Though I often travel as a result of my work, I’m always happy to come home to PE.
