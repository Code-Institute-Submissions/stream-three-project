About Site

Keryn Mason is a South African artist known for her unique style, which drapes a layer of fantasy over subjects of realism. The result is a collection of paintings that fall somewhere in between magical realism and photographic precision. A lover of the natural spectacle offered by her homeland of South Africa, Keryn’s works often feature wildlife and nature, but branch out to cover other subjects, such as portraits and abstract works, on occasion.

This site allows you to view and purchase Keryn’s works. You are also invited to visit the forum in order to discuss Keryn’s works as well as participate in general discussions about art and other artists. However, in order for us to monitor the forum content and ensure that your purchases are completed securely, we do ask you to register and sign in before you can enjoy these features.
