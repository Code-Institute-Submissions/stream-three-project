Valli M.

Sometimes animals are able to convey such boldness in their natures that they cause us as humans to stand up and take note. Valli M. is one such animal, and this portrait of him at Addo Elephant Park attempts to capture that bold nature. The original painting ‘Valli M. his early years at Addo’ was completed for Nicola Schwim, whose love and passion for Valli M. inspired its inception. The photograph that acted as a reference can be found in her book, ‘Elephant Footprints’, which I found to be an inspirational read.
-Keryn Mason
